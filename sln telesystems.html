Our Products

Dome Camera

A dome camera is a type of security camera that is housed in a dome-shaped casing. This design makes it less obtrusive and allows for discreet surveillance. Dome cameras are commonly used in both indoor and outdoor settings and are popular for their wide-angle coverage and resistance to tampering.

Bullet Camera

A bullet camera is a type of security camera shaped like a bullet. It is ideal for outdoor surveillance, offering directional monitoring with a compact design.

PTZ Camera

A PTZ camera (Pan-Tilt-Zoom camera) is a type of surveillance camera that can be remotely controlled to pan (move horizontally), tilt (move vertically), and zoom in or out. This allows it to cover a wide area and focus on specific details when needed.

Bio-Metric

Biometric refers to the measurement and statistical analysis of people's unique physical and behavioral characteristics. In the context of security and identification, biometric systems are used to recognize or verify a person's identity based on these traits.

UPS

A UPS (Uninterruptible Power Supply) is an electrical device that provides emergency power to a load when the main power source fails. It ensures that electronic equipment remains powered during outages or voltage fluctuations, allowing for safe shutdown or continuous operation.
